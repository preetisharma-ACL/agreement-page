Campaign Execution, Delivery & Refund Agreement

Between AAJneeti Connect Ltd. (ACL) and -

Campaign Setup & Go-Live Timeline

ACL requires 3–5 working days from the date of onboarding/payment to:

  • Set up campaign structure
  • Creatives & ad copies
  • Targeting & tracking systems

Campaign performance begins only after go-live, not from the date of payment.

Campaign Stabilization & Optimization Period (First 7 Days Post Go-Live)

The first 7 days after go-live are considered a learning and optimization phase.

During this phase:

  • Audience targeting is refined
  • Platform algorithms stabilize
  • Lead quality gradually improves

Clients may experience:

  • Higher number of non-interested leads
  • Lower initial qualification ratio

This is normal and expected in digital campaigns.

No refund, replacement, validation, or performance claims will be applicable during this period.

Refund, Validation & Replacement Applicability Timeline

  • Setup Period: 3–5 working days
  • Learning Phase: First 7 days post go-live
  • Buffer Period: Day 8–10

Validation, replacement, refund eligibility, and performance evaluation will begin strictly from Day 11 onwards (from date of go-live).

Any claims raised before Day 11 will not be considered.

Scope of Services

This Agreement governs the refund, replacement, and validation terms for:

  • Lead Generation Campaigns
  • Digital Marketing Campaigns
  • Performance Marketing Services

ACL commits to delivering leads and qualified leads as per agreed campaign scope and qualification benchmarks, not guaranteed sales, site visits, or closures.

Lead Delivery & Tracking Mechanism

All leads will be shared with the Client in real time via:

  • Google Sheets, or
  • CRM system

The Client is responsible for:

  • Contacting each lead
  • Updating lead status
  • Maintaining accurate feedback records

All evaluations will be based strictly on system records.

24-Hour Lead Response & Feedback Policy (Mandatory)

The Client must:

  • Contact each lead within 24 working hours of delivery
  • Update feedback within the same 24-hour period

If not complied:

  • The lead will be treated as auto-qualified
  • The lead will be treated as final and billable
  • The lead will not be eligible for validation, replacement, or dispute

Lead Qualification Rate Commitment

ACL operates on a committed qualification ratio model (e.g., 40%, 50%, 60% as agreed).

A lead is considered qualified if it is:

  • Relevant to the project
  • Genuinely interested
  • Contactable

Qualification does not guarantee conversion or sale.

Performance Evaluation & Billing Adjustment (From Day 11 Onwards)

Performance is evaluated on overall campaign data, not daily output.

Example:

Total leads delivered: 100 Agreed qualification rate: 50% Expected qualified leads: 50
Actual qualified leads delivered: 35 Shortfall: 15 qualified leads To maintain the agreed ratio:
35 qualified leads correspond to 70 total leads Final Calculation: Billable leads: 70
Non-billable leads: 30 Credit/Adjustment: 30 leads

✔ The Client pays only for effective lead volume

Lead Validation & Cross-Verification Process

Day 1 to Day 10:

  • Leads will be counted for overall qualification
  • Only qualified leads will be treated as final
  • Leads marked as “Not Qualified” will be reviewed internally
  • Not eligible for replacement

Day 11 Onwards:

  • Leads marked within 24 hours will be eligible for validation.
  • If found Qualified: Proof and call recordings may be shared
  • Lead will be treated as valid
  • If found Not Qualified: A replacement lead will be provided

ACL’s validation decision after review will be considered final.

Lead Classification & Replacement Eligibility

Replacement Applicable if:

  • Lead shared the wrong number
  • Not reachable (after reasonable attempts)
  • Completely irrelevant
  • Duplicate leads
  • Broker leads
  • Spam / bot leads

Not Eligible for Replacement:

  • Not interested
  • Call later / busy
  • Leads not contacted within 24 hours
  • Leads marked after 24 hours
  • Subjective rejection without verifiable proof

Failure to follow the 24-hour rule will result in automatic qualification.

Replacement Policy

  • Replacement will be provided only if:
  • Lead is received on or after Day 11
  • Marked within 24 hours
  • Proper feedback is updated
  • Verified by ACL as genuinely invalid

Delivery Commitment & Make-Good Policy

ACL is committed to delivering the agreed qualification benchmark and effective output.

If a shortfall occurs, ACL will:

  • Optimize campaigns
  • Deliver additional leads
  • Replace invalid leads

Until the agreed qualification commitment is met.

Refund Policy

ACL follows a performance-adjusted delivery model.

Primary Resolution:

  • Additional lead delivery
  • Replacement
  • Billing adjustment

Refund Eligibility:

If, after the agreed campaign period:

  • The Client chooses not to continue, and
  • A qualification shortfall still exists

Refund/credit will be processed based on non-billable leads.

Refund will not apply if:

  • 24-hour policy is not followed
  • Leads are auto-qualified
  • Client delays impact performance

Lock-In Period (No Refund Window)

If a 30-day campaign period is agreed:

  • No refund request can be raised during this period
  • Performance will be balanced through optimization and delivery.

Optimization Dependency Clause

Campaign performance depends on:

  • Timely Client follow-up
  • Accurate feedback
  • Coordination and approvals

Client delays will not be considered grounds for dispute.

ACL does not guarantee:

  • Sales
  • Site visits
  • Closures
